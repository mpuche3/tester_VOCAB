Welcome to Life in the UK Test (3000+)!

This app contains more than 3000 questions to help you prepare successfully for your Life in the UK test.

The app adapts to your learning progress by adjusting the frequency of questions based on your performance. If you get a question wrong, it will appear more often to help reinforce your understanding. If you answer correctly, that question will appear less frequently, allowing you to focus on the areas where you need the most improvement. This personalised approach ensures an optimised learning process, making your preparation more efficient and effective.

Tip: Click the button in the top right to activate voice. This feature allows the app to read questions and explanations aloud, helping you reinforce your learning through listening as well.

Good luck with your preparation!
